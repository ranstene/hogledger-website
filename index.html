Piggery Accounting

Record income and expenses, attach receipt photos, link new records to active pigs or cages, and keep stock purchases farm-wide until usage is logged.

Livestock & Cage Records

Manage tagged pigs, cage assignments, photos, weight history, sale readiness, mortality records, sales, and purchase history.

Photo Attachments

Attach camera or gallery photos to pigs, transactions, medicine, and vitamin records for clearer proof and history.

Feed & Stock Control

Track feed inventory, supplier purchases, consumption, expiry dates, and low-stock alerts. Feed purchases stay farm-wide, while consumption allocates cost to cages.

Health Calendar

Log medicines and vitamins with photos, manage due doses from one shared log form, track stock usage, and monitor withdrawal safe dates.

Daily Operations

Use the Today tab for priorities across feed, low stock, health calendar reminders, disinfection, and backup checks.

Smart Reminders

Manage notification preferences for treatments, vitamins, withdrawal periods, disinfection, low stock, and backup reminders.

Reports & Backups

Review action plans, cage profit, feed usage, medicine and vitamin costs, disinfection, mortality, CSV/PDF exports, and full farm backup files.
