Piggery Accounting

Record income and expenses, connect costs to pigs or cages, and review profit with current currency settings.

Livestock & Cage Records

Manage tagged pigs, cage or pen assignments, weights, photos, mortality records, sales, and purchase history.

Feed & Stock Control

Track feed inventory, supplier purchases, consumption, expiry dates, low-stock alerts, and automatic expense entries.

Health Schedules

Log medicines and vitamins, manage multi-dose treatment schedules, and monitor withdrawal periods before sale.

Daily Operations

Use the Today tab for priorities across feed, low stock, treatment reminders, disinfection, and backup checks.

Reports & Backups

Review financial reports, cage performance, expense breakdowns, and export or import full JSON backups.

Offline-First by Design

Your Farm Data Stays on Your Device

HogLedger stores farm records locally with SQLite, so daily accounting, livestock, feed, medicine, breeding, and disinfection records remain available without internet.
