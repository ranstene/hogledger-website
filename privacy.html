Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Overview

HogLedger is an offline-first piggery accounting and farm operations app. It is designed to help users record farm finances, livestock, feed, medicine, vitamins, health calendar tasks, breeding, disinfection, reports, and backups.

The app is built so your farm records are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export, share, or import backup files yourself.

Information You Enter

HogLedger may store information you enter into the app, including livestock records, transactions, feed inventory and usage, health and medicine records, vitamin schedules, treatment schedules, withdrawal safe dates, breeding records, disinfection records, photos, farm profile details, settings, and backup history.

Local Storage

HogLedger stores app data locally on your device. Daily app use does not require an internet connection, and the app does not automatically upload your farm records to a cloud server.

Backups and Exports

The app may let you export or restore farm backup files. If you export, send, store, or share a backup file outside the app, you are responsible for where that file goes and who can access it.

Photos and Files

If you attach photos or import files, those items may be stored with your local farm records. The app may request access to device file or photo features only when needed for app functions such as attachments, import, or export.

Notifications

HogLedger may use local notifications for reminders such as Health Calendar tasks, treatments, vitamins, withdrawal periods, disinfection, low stock, and backups. Notification settings can be managed in the app or through your device settings.

No Sale of Personal Data

HogLedger does not sell your farm records or personal data.

Data Security

You are responsible for protecting your device, app access, exported backups, and any shared files. Use device lock features and store backup files in a secure location.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because HogLedger stores farm records locally, your app data remains on your device until you delete records in the app, clear the app data, uninstall the app, or replace it during a restore. Exported backup files remain wherever you choose to save or share them until you delete those files.

For privacy questions or deletion-related support, contact tamdevs.support@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

HogLedger is intended for farm owners, farm managers, and agricultural users. It is not directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as HogLedger changes. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact tamdevs.support@gmail.com.
