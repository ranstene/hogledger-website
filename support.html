Help for backup, restore, livestock, finance, feed, Health Calendar, disinfection, reports, and app setup.

Contact Support

For help, questions, or bug reports, email tamdevs.support@gmail.com.

Please include your device model, Android version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Before You Contact Us

If the app looks stuck or records do not refresh after restoring a backup, fully close and reopen HogLedger.

If you are reporting a problem, screenshots are helpful. Do not send private farm backup files unless support specifically asks for one.

Backup and Restore

Use Create Backup regularly to save a copy of your farm records. Keep backup files somewhere safe, such as your own cloud storage or external drive.

Use Replace All when moving to a new phone or restoring after reinstalling. Use Add to Existing only when combining records from another device or farm file.

Local Data

HogLedger is offline-first. Farm records are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export, share, or restore backup files.

Photos

Photos attached in the app are kept with local records and included in newer backup files. Large photo collections can make backup files bigger.

Notifications

If reminders are not appearing, check that notifications are enabled in HogLedger settings and in your Android device settings.

The Health Calendar combines medicine treatment schedules, vitamin schedules, and withdrawal safe dates. Calendar items can open the shared medicine/vitamin log form, so stock usage and costs are recorded in the same place as the Usage Log.

Stock Purchases and Cage Costs

Feed, medicine, vitamin, and disinfectant stock purchases are kept farm-wide. Costs are assigned to cages or individual pigs when stock is used in Operations or Health & Medicine.

New transaction and log pickers show active pigs and cages with active pigs. Historical records for sold or deceased pigs remain available in reports and history.
